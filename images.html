HOW DOES CYBERSLAP WORK?

When you first open the app, pick a username. Once that's done, you'll land on the main screen with two options: Users & Communities or Send A Slap.

On the Users & Communities page, you'll see your username and a Join box.
Type the exact name of the community you want and hit the button.

Go to Send A Slap — you'll see all the communities you're in.
Tap one → pick your victim → write your roast → hit send.

The victim now gets non-stop heads-up notifications every 10 seconds until they accept the slap.
No escape. No mercy.

When they finally accept? Full-screen slap animation, loud slap sound, and your personal message displayed in all its glory.
